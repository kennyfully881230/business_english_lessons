Business English: "Hold Your Horses"

Welcome to today's business English lesson!
欢迎参加今天的商务英语课程！
Today we'll learn about the expression: "Hold your horses"
今天我们将学习这个表达："Hold your horses"（稍安勿躁）
This is a common phrase used in professional settings when someone needs to slow down or wait.
这是在专业场合常用的短语，当需要某人放慢速度或等待时使用。

Literal Meaning

"Hold your horses" literally means to keep control of your horses.
"Hold your horses" 字面意思是控制住你的马匹。
It comes from times when people rode horses or used horse-drawn carriages.
它源自人们骑马或使用马车的时代。
When horses were excited or moving too fast, the rider would need to "hold" them back.
当马匹兴奋或移动太快时，骑手需要"拉住"它们。

Modern Meaning

Today, it means: "Wait a moment", "Slow down", or "Be patient"
如今，它的意思是："等一下"，"慢点"，或"耐心点"
It's used when someone is acting too quickly or needs to wait for more information.
当某人行动太快或需要等待更多信息时使用。
Example: "Hold your horses! We need to check the contract details before signing."
例子："别着急！我们需要先检查合同细节再签字。"

When to Use It

Appropriate situations in business:
商务中的适用场景：
  • When a colleague wants to make a quick decision without all the facts
    当同事想要在没有所有事实的情况下快速做决定时
  • When someone is rushing through a presentation or meeting
    当有人在演示或会议中过于匆忙时
  • When you need more time to prepare something
    当你需要更多时间准备某事时

How to Use It Professionally

This is an informal expression. In very formal situations, you might say:
这是一个非正式表达。在非常正式的场合，你可以说：
"Let's pause for a moment to review the details."
"让我们暂停一下，审查细节。"
But in most business situations, "hold your horses" is acceptable when said politely.
但在大多数商务场合，礼貌地说"hold your horses"是可以接受的。
"I appreciate your enthusiasm, but let's hold our horses until we get the financial report."
"我欣赏你的热情，但我们还是等拿到财务报告再说。"

Practice Examples

Try to understand these business situations:
试着理解这些商务场景：
Colleague: "I'll email the client right now with our new offer!"
You: "Hold your horses! The marketing team hasn't approved the final pricing yet."
同事："我现在就给客户发邮件提供我们的新报价！"
你："别急！市场部还没批准最终定价呢。"
Manager: "We should launch the product next week!"
You: "Let's hold our horses until we complete the quality testing."
经理："我们应该下周就推出产品！"
你："我们还是等完成质量测试再说吧。"

Role Play Exercise

Practice with a partner. One person is eager to act, the other says "hold your horses":
与伙伴练习。一个人急于行动，另一个人说"hold your horses"：
Situation 1: A colleague wants to send a contract before legal review.
情景1：同事想在法律审查前发送合同。
Situation 2: Your boss wants to hire a candidate after the first interview.
情景2：你的老板想在第一次面试后就雇佣候选人。
Situation 3: A team member wants to present unfinished data to investors.
情景3：团队成员想向投资者展示未完成的数据。

Review

Remember:
记住：
  • "Hold your horses" means to wait or slow down
    "Hold your horses"意思是等待或放慢速度
  • It's informal but acceptable in most business situations
    这是非正式表达，但在大多数商务场合可以接受
  • Use it when someone is acting too quickly without necessary information
    当有人在缺乏必要信息时行动太快时使用
  • In very formal situations, use alternatives like "let's pause" or "let's wait"
    在非常正式的场合，使用替代表达如"let's pause"或"let's wait"

Thank You!

Now you know how to use "hold your horses" in business English!
现在你学会了如何在商务英语中使用"hold your horses"！
Try using it in your next meeting when someone is moving too quickly.
当下次会议中有人行动太快时，尝试使用它。
Remember to always say it with a polite tone and smile.
记住总是用礼貌的语气和微笑来说这句话。
